Comets

Haley's Comet

Halley's Comet is the only known short-period comet that is consistently visible to the naked eye from Earth, appearing every 72–80 years.

Great Comet

a sung-through musical adaptation of a 70-page segment from Leo Tolstoy's 1869 novel War and Peace. The show was written by composer.

Ikeya Comet

formally designated C/1965 S1, 1965 VIII, and 1965f, was a long-period comet discovered independently by Kaoru Ikeya and Tsutomu Seki.

Hayakutake Comet

this is also known as Comet C/1996 B2, was a bright, long-tailed comet that was visible in the night sky in 1996. It was discovered on January 30, 1996.
